Here are some projects!

Massachusetts Music Venues

Ongoing, self-imposed effort to add every music venue in Massachusetts (past and present) to Wikidata. This site is built with Snowman, a static site generator for SPARQL backends.

Boston-area Amateur Musical Ensembles

At some point, I'll turn this into a legit site, I swear. For now, a potentially useful collection of information about community bands and orchestras around Boston.

Les Shortparisiens

On hiatus—at some point, I want to move this off Wordpress! We'll see! Fan site for the band Shortparis, started when I was in library school and thinking a lot about fan archival practices thanks to this 2018 article about the Tragically Hip in Archivaria and also the Soviet rock underground (and also parallels between the 1980s and 2010s St. Petersburg music scenes...)
